Maryann Soper BSc (hons) MA

Values-led Digital Lead

Heritage Ability Project Manager

Living Options Devon · Nov 2017 – Aug 2019

As Project Manager for the award‑winning Heritage Ability programme, I led digital accessibility improvements across 23 heritage sites, ensuring disabled visitors could engage with culture, history, and nature through inclusive digital tools and experiences.

  • Designed and implemented digital solutions for disabled visitors across heritage sites of national significance.
  • Delivered accessible digital products including Easy Read guides with 360 videos, British Sign Language videos, and audio tours.
  • Led the “Vote with your Heart” campaign, which won a National Lottery Award.
  • Created an underground mining VR experience for people with disabilities.
  • Managed multi‑site delivery, stakeholder engagement, and accessibility compliance.

In the heart of the central team, I was employed to set up and manage the new Knowledge Hub, an internal knowledge base, enabling the workforce of 250+ to access and deliver grant-making processes, information, resources and management systems. I employed both traditional and Agile project management to dove-tail digital developments with the rest of the organisation. With a Master’s in Heritage Management and experience as a trustee, I also provided continuous professional development for colleagues. I contracted specialist consultants to co-deliver information and training on subjects such as D/deaf, Sight loss and Creating Accessible Documents. I specialised in developing digital solutions and led on the WCAG 2.1 AA digital communications standard.

Additionally, I created two intermediate Process Centres to receive funding applications throughout the pandemic for non-lottery applications. These were used by the entire organisation to administrate grant-in-aid money provided by the Government, in conjunction with Historic England and DEFRA. I also created a Portfolio Management Service (in a live environment) allowing grant-makers to manage their personal portfolio of work, as well as regional teams to report to executives on resourcing and investment levels.

I also developed quick ‘proof of concepts’ using Microsoft tools for user testing and concept analysis, such as the Capital Data Analysis Tool and Consultant Zone, for collaboration with external agencies. I am completely conversant in Microsoft technologies and have completed formal training in PowerBI and a Masterclass in SharePoint Online, as well as Agile Project Management.
