National Lottery Heritage Fund July 2019 - Sept 2022

The National Lottery Heritage Fund is the largest funder for the UK’s heritage. Using money raised by National Lottery players, they distribute grants from £10,000 to £10million to support projects across the UK that connect people and communities to their heritage. I sat in the central team funtion supporting all nations to fairly distribute funds in a heavily regulated environment and during the covid pandemic. I cascaded the vision of the policy makers throughout the organisation. I employed both traditional and Agile methodologies to be able to dove-tail digital developments with legacy systems.

Inclusion Zone

I created the Inclusion Zone to support grant-makers to understand a wider range of people in order to fairly distribute funds and meet a key priority for the organisation.
